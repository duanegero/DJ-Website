DJ Push N' Play

ABOUT

With over a decade of experience in the music scene, Push N Play has been spinning tracks and setting the vibe for 10 plus years. Specializing in R&B, Rap/Hip-Hop, Reggae, 90's and old school, Push N Play brings a unique blend of nostalgic beats and contemporary rhythms that resonates with an adult crowd.

Whether it's a lively birthday party, a large-scale event, or an intimate gathering, Push N Play knows how to tailor the music to create the perfect atmosphere. With a keen ear for mixing and an intuitive understanding of the crowd's energy, Push N Play ensures that every event is unforgettable.

From the classics that take you back to the latest hits that get everyone on the dance floor, Push N Play crafts a musical journey that keeps the audience engaged and entertained. Known for professionalism, reliability, and a passion for music, Push N Play is your go-to DJ for any occasion.
